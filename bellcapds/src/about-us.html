What We Do

 

The Bellarmine Career and Professional Development Society works to help current students learn about different industries and begin developing their professional profiles. We do this by coordinating guest speakers for our weekly lunch meetings, hosting a yearly Career Fair, teaching workshop sessions like Resume building or creating a LinkedIn profile, and much more.
